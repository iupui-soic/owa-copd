Clinical practice guideline recommendation #1

The guideline panel recommends prescribing inhaled short-acting beta 2-agonists (SABAs) to patients with confirmed COPD for rescue therapy as needed

Clinical practice guideline recommendation #2

The guideline panel recommends offering long-acting bronchodilators to patients with confirmed, stable COPD who continue to have respiratory symptoms

Clinical practice guideline recommendation #3
